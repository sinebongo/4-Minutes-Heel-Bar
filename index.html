We Provide Quality Dry Cleaning, Watch And Shoe Repair And Refurbishment Services

We only take 4 minutes to fix it!

4 minutes heelbar is a full-service garment care and dry cleaning company, also provide watchmaking and shoe repair and refurbishment services, founded in Gauteng South, on the simple idea that your shoes, watch and garment are your first impressions and they deserves the highest quality care as it is a reflection of your own personal style. 4 minutes heelbar is a vibrant startup and is family-owned and operated business in the South of Gauteng with Thabile Mahlangu, Khanyisile Mahlangu and a few other employees. We operate remotely , you schedule a pick up with us and we will collect and deliver your items.
